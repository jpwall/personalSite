Who am I?

As stated earlier, my name is Jesse Philip Walling and I am a high school student living in Seattle, Washington. I am currently a sophomore (16 years old) attending Seattle Academy of Arts and Sciences, more commonly known as SAAS. Some of my burning passions include design, computers, and art. At my school I participate in the robotics program where a large amount of design, prototyping, problem solving, and programming occur at once. This is one of my favorite things to do because it perfectly aligns with my interests in design and computers. I use linux daily and only use Windows when it is absolutely necessary. Other than that, I would rather not participate in proprietary software projects. Take a look at my projects page if you want to learn more about my (light) programming endeavors. Finally, I enjoy doing art as well. Along with taking art class by choice at school, I do some portrait drawing at home. You can take a look at all of them by going to my projects page.
