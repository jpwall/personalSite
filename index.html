I am currently a sophomore attending Seattle Academy of Arts and Sciences, more commonly known as SAAS. Some of my burning passions include design, computers in general, and art. At my school I participate in the robotics program where a large amount of design, prototyping, problem solving, and programming all occur at once. This is one of my favorite things to do because it perfectly aligns with my interests in design and computers. Other than that, I would rather not participate in an oversized business earning money off of people just so that they can use a computer. Take a look at my projects page if you want to learn more about my (light) programming endeavors so far in my life. Finally, I enjoy doing art as well.
