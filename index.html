Who are you?

Well, my full name is Jesse Philip Walling, and I am currently a junior in high school (16 years old) attending Seattle Academy of Arts and Sciences, more commonly known as SAAS. I am passionate about computers, design, and art. At my school I participate in the robotics program where a large majority of my interests meld together in one place.
